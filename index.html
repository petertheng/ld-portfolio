Sample Project: Anatomy & Physiology Curriculum Design

Challenge

The school lacked an established Anatomy & Physiology course. No standardized resources, assessments, or content existed for either Honors or Non-Honors tracks.

Solution

  • Used ADDIE and Bloom's Taxonomy to design a comprehensive curriculum
  • Developed real-world case studies and project-based learning modules
  • Created hands-on labs including dissections and research-based assignments
  • Implemented digital tools for assessments and engagement

Tools Used

  • Canvas, Google Slides, Nearpod, YouTube Studio
  • Kahoot, Gimkit, Edpuzzle, Google Docs/Sheets

Role

  • Defined learning objectives aligned with standards
  • Created multimedia content and authentic assessments
  • Facilitated collaboration and peer learning
  • Used data analytics to iterate and improve instruction

Results

Students scored 8–12% higher than district averages on the final exam over a 9-year period. Significantly increased learner engagement and achievement.
